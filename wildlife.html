About

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is spotting wildlife guaranteed?
Spotting wildlife is never 100% guaranteed as it depends on nature, season, and luck. However, our guides maximize your chances with their expertise.
What wildlife are we expected to see in Jordan?
Jordan is home to vast wildlife, including Arabian oryx, water buffalo, raptors, sea turtles, desert foxes, Nubian ibex, striped hyenas, sand cats, and even whale sharks.
When is the best time to visit Jordan to look for wildlife?
The best time is spring (March–May) and autumn (September–November), when temperatures are pleasant and wildlife activity is high.
